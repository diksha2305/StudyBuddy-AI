# StudySmart AI: Intelligence Platform Overview

> **"Where curiosity meets artificial intelligence to spark absolute brilliance."**

StudySmart AI is a state-of-the-art, premium academic productivity ecosystem. It transforms traditional study workflows into a high-performance, AI-augmented experience, combining a specialized AI tutor, autonomous scheduling, and industrial-grade obsidian aesthetics.

---

## 🎨 The "Deep Obsidian" Design System
StudySmart utilizes a custom-engineered **Obsidian Premium** design system, built on **Tailwind CSS 4**:
- **Backgrounds**: Deep, solid obsidian surfaces (`#05070a` / `#0a0e14`) optimized for maximum visual focus and data clarity.
- **Typography**: Globally standardized to **Inter** and **Plus Jakarta Sans** for zero-latency readability and a modern academic feel.
- **Branding (Neural Hub)**: A circular, floating "Neural Hub" logo with integrated neon glowing architecture and smooth physics-based animations.
- **Dynamic Indicators**: High-intensity neon accents (Electric Cyan, Deep Indigo, Vivid Purple) used for priority targets and strategic insights.

---

## 🛠️ Core Technology Stack
- **Framework**: [Next.js 16.2.1](https://nextjs.org/) (App Router + Turbopack)
- **AI Core**: [Groq SDK](https://wow.groq.com/) for near-instant (Llama 3.1) reasoning.
- **Voice Intelligence**: [Twilio Voice Agent](https://www.twilio.com/) for autonomous deadline reminders and voice interaction.
- **Styling Engine**: [Tailwind CSS 4](https://tailwindcss.com/) with a fully customized `@theme` block.

---

## 📂 Prime Modules & Intelligence Hubs

### 1. **Command Center (StudyPlanner)**
The brain of the operation. It simulates 1,200+ study paths to find the highest score-per-hour trajectory.
- **Agentic Voice Caller**: If a deadline is missed by 5+ minutes, the AI autonomously initiates a phone call for immediate calibration.
- **Industrial TaskCards**: 2px-solid bordered components with neon priority indicators.

### 2. **Neural Hub (Chat AI)**
A persistent floating AI assistant calibrated for real-time concept synthesis, fully integrated with your study history and current targets.

### 3. **Strategic Insights (Dashboard)**
Real-time visualization of "Strategic Depth," core mastery percentages, and concept resolution counts (e.g., "Top 4% Global Elite").
